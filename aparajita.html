Butterfly Pea Tea (Clitoria ternatea) — parampara aur research
Jaankari ke liye · ilaaj ke liye nahi

Aaj ki chai 🔵

Antioxidant lab

Neele rang ke liye zimmedar ternatins (anthocyanins) aur kaempferol — ye free radicals se hone wale nuksan se bachav mein madad karte hain.

Sugar control animal chhota human

Khoon mein shakkar ke avshoshan (absorption) ko niyantrit karne mein madad ka sanket. Kuch chhote manav adhyayan bhi hue hain — par abhi seemit.

Soojan kam karna (anti-inflammatory) lab

Test-tube adhyayanon mein soojan kam karne wale gun mile hain.

Yaaddasht / dimaag animal

Chuhon par shodh mein acetylcholine (yaaddasht se juda rasayan) badha — par yeh insaanon par siddh nahi hua hai. Isliye "memory booster" kehna jaldbaazi hogi.

Baal aur twacha human

2024 ke ek adhyayan mein 20% Clitoria ternatea wale shampoo ne rusi (dandruff) kam ki — halke side effects ke saath. Baal badhne mein madad ke bhi sanket hain.

Imaandari se: zyadatar shodh test-tube aur chuhon par hua hai — insaanon par bade clinical trials abhi kam hain. Isliye ise swasth aadat maanein, dawa nahi.
1.1 cup garam paani mein 5–8 sukhe aparajita phool daalein.
2.Dhak kar 5 minute rakhein — paani gehra neela ho jayega. Chhaan lein.
3.Nimbu ki kuch boondein daaliye — chai turant gulabi/baingani ho jayegi (anthocyanins pH se rang badalte hain). Bachchon ko yeh bahut pasand aata hai!
4.Swad halka hota hai. Caffeine bilkul nahi — shaam ya raat ko bhi pi sakte hain. Cheeni na daalein to behtar.
Yeh app sirf jaankari ke liye hai — kisi doctor ki salah, jaanch ya ilaaj ka vikalp nahi. Aparajita ke zyadatar faayde abhi lab aur jaanwaron par hue shodh se hain, insaanon par nahi. Isliye kisi bhi bimari (sugar, BP, yaaddasht) ke ilaaj ke liye ise dawa ki jagah na lein. Dawa chal rahi ho, garbhavastha ho, ya koi purani bimari ho — to apne doctor se poochh kar hi shuru karein.
