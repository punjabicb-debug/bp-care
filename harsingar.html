Nyctanthes arbor-tristis — Night Jasmine
Parampara aur research · sirf jaankari ke liye

Pehle ek imaandar baat

Harsingar par baaki phoolon se thodi zyada manav-research hui hai — khaaskar jodon ke dard (arthritis), bukhaar aur dengue mein platelet ke liye. Phir bhi iski koi tay-shuda "roz ki chai" wali surakshit dose sthapit nahi hai. Isliye is app mein bhi koi daily tracker nahi — yeh sirf batati hai ki parampara aur vigyan kya kehte hain.

Jodon ka dard — arthritis parampara human

Harsingar ke patton ka kaadha sciatica aur gathiya (arthritis) ke liye Ayurved mein sabse mashhoor upyog hai. Kuch chhote manav-adhyayanon mein dard aur soojan kam hone ke sanket mile — par bade, pukhta trials abhi kam hain.

Bukhaar aur dengue platelet animal human

Patton ke ras ka upyog paramparik roop se bukhaar mein hota hai. Kuch chhote adhyayanon mein dengue ke mareezon mein platelet count sudharne ke sanket mile — yeh abhi pukhta siddh nahi, doctor ke ilaaj ka vikalp bilkul nahi.

Soojan aur antioxidant animal lab

Patton aur phoolon ke extract mein anti-inflammatory aur free-radical se bachav wala asar — jaanwaron aur lab mein lagataar milta hai.

Sugar (blood glucose) animal

Chuhon par extract ne khoon ki shakkar kam ki (hypoglycemic asar). Insaanon par yeh siddh nahi hua.

Anti-malarial / anti-parasitic lab animal

Test-tube aur chuhon mein malaria ke parjeevi ke khilaf asar mila hai.

Anti-microbial / anti-fungal lab

Kuch bacteria aur fungus ke khilaf test-tube mein asar.

Harsingar mein mukhya roop se iridoid glycosides aur flavonoids hote hain — yahi iske asar ke peechhe hain:

Inhi mein se arbortristoside par sabse zyada shodh hua hai — mukhya roop se soojan, bukhaar aur parjeevi ke liye. Naarangi rang ke danthal (calyx) se paramparik peela-kesari rang bhi banta hai.

🌙Raat ki rani ka bhai: Harsingar ke phool raat mein khilte hain aur subah tak jhad jaate hain — isi liye "arbor-tristis" yaani "udaas ped". Khushbu poori raat faili rehti hai.
🕉Pauranik katha: Parijat ko swarg ka vriksh mana jata hai — samudra manthan se nikla. Krishna-Satyabhama ki katha isi phool se judi hai.
🎨Kesari rang: Phool ke naarangi danthal se paramparik peela rang banta hai — kabhi reshmi kapdon ki rangaai mein upyog hota tha.
🌳Poore Bharat mein aam — mandir, aangan aur bageechon mein. West Bengal ka rajya-pushp bhi hai.
Yeh app sirf jaankari ke liye hai — doctor ki salah, jaanch ya ilaaj ka vikalp nahi.

• Harsingar ki surakshit roz ki maatra insaanon ke liye tay nahi hai. Apne mann se rozana kaadha ya ras shuru na karein.
• Dengue ya tez bukhaar mein Harsingar ko doctor ke ilaaj ka vikalp bilkul na samjhein — platelet girna jaanleva ho sakta hai, turant doctor ke paas jaayein.
• Sugar, BP ya koi bhi dawa chal rahi ho — to Harsingar bilkul na lein jab tak doctor ya yogya vaidya se salah na le lein (sugar ki dawa ke saath shakkar zyada gir sakti hai).
• Garbhavastha, stanpaan, ya bachchon ko dena ho — to bina vaidya ki salah ke nahi.
