1. Vasodilation — naliyan khulti hain

Nitric oxide banata hai, jisse blood vessels khul kar lachile (pliable) hote hain.

Promotes nitric oxide → vessels widen & become more pliable.

2. Diuretic — extra paani/namak bahar

Natural diuretic ki tarah kaam karke shareer se atirikt sodium nikalti hai.

Acts as a natural diuretic → helps lower pressure.

3. Natural ACE inhibitor

Angiotensin II (jo naliyan sankuchit karta hai) banne se rokti hai — waise hi jaise BP ki kuch dawaiyan.

Blocks angiotensin II, the hormone that narrows vessels.

4. Anti-inflammatory — naliyan naram rahein

Chronic inflammation kam karke dhamniyon (arteries) ko samay ke saath sakht hone se bachati hai.

Reduces chronic inflammation → prevents artery stiffening over time.
•Do cup roz: research kehti hai din mein 2 cup se systolic aur diastolic dono mein achhi kami aati hai.
•Shaam ke liye acchi: zyadatar hibiscus chai caffeine-free hoti hai, isliye raat ko relax karne wala drink ban sakti hai. Label zaroor check karein ki added caffeine na ho.
•Kidney: aam taur par kidney ke liye safe maani jaati hai, aur kuch CKD wale log bhi BP ke liye istemal karte hain — par apne doctor se confirm karke hi shuru karein.
Yeh jaankari sirf educational hai — kisi doctor ki salah, jaanch ya ilaaj ka vikalp nahi. Agar BP ki dawa chal rahi ho ya kidney ki koi samasya ho, toh hibiscus chai shuru karne se pehle apne physician se zaroor baat karein. Dawa apne aap band ya kam na karein.
