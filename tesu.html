Butea monosperma — Flame of the Forest
Parampara aur research · sirf jaankari ke liye

Pehle ek imaandar baat

Gudhal aur aparajita ki tarah Tesu ki koi tay-shuda roz ki chai nahi hai — iski surakshit maatra (dose) insaanon ke liye sthapit nahi hui. Iski lagbhag saari research abhi lab aur chuhon par hai. Isliye is app mein koi "roz 2 cup" wala tracker nahi hai — yeh sirf batati hai ki parampara aur vigyan kya kehte hain.

Soojan aur antioxidant animal lab

Sabse zyada aur sabse lagataar milne wala parinaam — phool ke flavonoids soojan (inflammation) kam karte hain aur free radicals se bachav karte hain.

Sugar (blood glucose) animal

Chuhon par kiye adhyayanon mein Butea ke extract ne khoon ki shakkar kam ki (hypoglycemic asar). Insaanon par yeh siddh nahi hua.

Liver ki suraksha animal

Hepatoprotective asar — jaanwaron ke adhyayanon mein liver ko nuksan se bachav ke sanket.

Twacha — safed daag, psoriasis parampara lab

Ayurved mein vitiligo (safed daag) aur psoriasis ke liye lambe samay se upyog. Palash-tel bahari (topical) roop mein lagaya jata hai. Aadhunik manav-adhyayan abhi kam hain.

Anti-microbial / anti-fungal lab

Test-tube mein kuch bacteria aur fungus ke khilaf asar mila hai.

Pet aur pachan parampara

Paramparik roop se pet dard, pachan aur pet ke keede (krimi) ke liye upyog — yeh gyaan anubhav-aadharit hai, clinical trial se nahi.

🔥Flame of the Forest: Falgun-Chaitra mein poora ped naarangi-laal phoolon se dhak jata hai — isi liye ise "jungle ki aag" kehte hain.
🎨Holi ka asli rang: Tesu ke phoolon ko paani mein bhigokar banaya gaya kesari rang — Holi ka paramparik, rasayan-mukt rang. Aaj ke kritrim rangon se kahin surakshit.
🕉Ayurved mein: "Palash" ke phool, chhal, beej aur gond — sabka alag upyog. Phool mukhya roop se soojan, twacha aur pachan ke liye.
🌳Rajasthan, MP, Bihar, Jharkhand ke jungles mein aam. Rajya-vriksh bhi hai kuch jagah.
Yeh app sirf jaankari ke liye hai — doctor ki salah, jaanch ya ilaaj ka vikalp nahi.

• Tesu ki surakshit roz ki maatra insaanon ke liye tay nahi hai. Isliye apne mann se iski chai ya kaadha rozana na shuru karein.
• Sugar, BP ya koi bhi dawa chal rahi ho — to Tesu bilkul na lein jab tak doctor ya yogya vaidya se salah na le lein (sugar ki dawa ke saath milkar shakkar zyada gir sakti hai).
• Garbhavastha, stanpaan, ya bachchon ko dena ho — to bina vaidya ki salah ke nahi.
• Holi ka rang banane ke liye phoolon ka upyog surakshit aur paramparik hai — yeh alag baat hai.
